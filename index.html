About Alexander Auto Repair

At Alexander Auto Repair, we're dedicated to providing the Avon, Indiana community with honest, reliable, and professional automotive repair services. Our team of certified technicians has years of experience working on all makes and models.

We understand that your vehicle is an important investment, and we treat every car that comes through our doors with the same care and attention we'd give our own. From routine maintenance to complex repairs, we're committed to getting you back on the road safely and quickly.
